fjüled: f-yool-ed

noun: fuel

- food or drink as a source of energy

synonyms: nourishment, food, sustenance, nutriment, nutrition


There are many fuel options for athletes though few healthy and clean. Even fewer that cater to the specific energy and ergonomic needs of endurance athletes. Fjüled was created by a competitive triathlete who wasn't able to find simple, clean energy to fuel her through training and races.
